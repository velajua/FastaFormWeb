What FastaForm provides

FastaForm is an offline Android form-entry application for importing .fstfrm form definitions, filling records, saving drafts, amending submitted records, previewing data, exporting files, and generating scoped credentials.

Credential use

Credentials are scoped to matching form IDs and can unlock up to 3 forms. Users are responsible for storing, distributing, and tracking generated credentials after they are created. Do not share credentials outside the authorized team or project.
